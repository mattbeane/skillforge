# Exercise 1: Cross-Disciplinary Synthesis

## Context

Breakthrough insights often come from combining literatures that don't usually talk to each other. This exercise develops your ability to synthesize across disciplinary boundaries.

## The Challenge

You're writing a paper on **how organizations develop AI capabilities**. This topic touches multiple disciplines that rarely cite each other:

- **Strategy/Capabilities literature** (dynamic capabilities, resource-based view)
- **Computer science/ML literature** (technical implementation, ML ops)
- **Sociology/STS literature** (social shaping of technology, expertise)
- **Learning/HRD literature** (training, skill development)
