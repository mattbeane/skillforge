## Evaluation Criteria

### Passing (Foundation Badge Earned)

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Concept origins | Correctly identifies originator and original problem |
| Conceptual evolution | Identifies meaningful change over time |
| Debate mapping | Two genuinely distinct positions identified |
| Underlying disagreement | Goes beyond surface disagreement |
| Critical awareness | Recognizes limitations of citation-based reading |

### Common Mistakes

- Identifying positions that are really the same position
- Missing the deeper disagreement beneath empirical disputes
- Thinking all highly-cited papers represent the whole conversation
- Conflating popular press discussion with scholarly debate
