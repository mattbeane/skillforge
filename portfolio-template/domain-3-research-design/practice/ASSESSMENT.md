## Evaluation Criteria

### Passing (Practice Badge Earned)

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Design coherence | Design matches question and constraints |
| Measure validity | Outcomes are meaningfully operationalized |
| Threat awareness | Major identification problems identified |
| Honest calibration | Claim strength matches design strength |
| Defense quality | Responds substantively to critiques |

### Excellence Indicators

- Innovative identification strategy within constraints
- Multiple robustness approaches planned
- Heterogeneity analysis is theoretically motivated
- Defense shows deep understanding of limitations
- Acknowledges what ideal design would look like

### Common Mistakes

- Proposing experiments when they're not feasible
- Ignoring selection into work arrangements
- Over-claiming from observational data
- Weak operationalization of "work-life balance"
- Not planning for alternative explanations
