## Evaluation Criteria

### Passing (Foundation Badge Earned)

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Code/theme distinction | Correctly classifies at least 4/5 items |
| Theme construction | Themes are interpretive, not just categories |
| Saturation judgment | Recognizes limits of claimed saturation |
| Rigor understanding | Can articulate how qualitative research achieves rigor |

### Common Mistakes

- Treating all labels as equivalent (missing the abstraction levels)
- Confusing "no new codes" with theoretical saturation
- Unable to articulate what makes qualitative findings trustworthy
- Themes that are just relabeled code categories
