# Exercise 1: Claim-Evidence Alignment

## Context

## The Concept

Claims exist on a continuum from weak to strong:

| Claim Type | Language | Evidence Required |
|------------|----------|-------------------|
| Observational | "is associated with" | Correlation |
| Directional | "predicts", "leads to" | Correlation + theory |
| Causal | "causes", "affects" | Experimental or quasi-experimental |
| Universal | "always", "never" | Comprehensive evidence |
