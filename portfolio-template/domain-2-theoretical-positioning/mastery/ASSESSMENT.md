## Evaluation Criteria

### Passing (Mastery Badge Earned)

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Three distinct positionings | Genuinely different conversations, not just different journals |
| Strategic analysis | Considers novelty, competition, career, credibility |
| Decision quality | Choice is defensible given stated goals |
| Defense quality | Responds to challenges without becoming defensive or collapsing |
| Self-awareness | Acknowledges limitations and tradeoffs honestly |

### Excellence Indicators

- Positionings reference specific scholars and papers
- Identifies non-obvious connections between literatures
- Strategic analysis includes long-term career considerations
- Defense responses show genuine engagement with criticism
- Can articulate what would change their mind

### Failure Modes

- Positionings are superficially different (same argument, different words)
- Strategic analysis ignores career implications
- Cannot defend choice under pressure
- Treats positioning as purely intellectual (ignores politics/career)
- Unable to articulate opportunity costs
