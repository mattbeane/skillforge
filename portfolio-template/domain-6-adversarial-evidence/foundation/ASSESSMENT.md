## Evaluation Criteria

### Passing (Foundation Badge Earned)

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Disconfirmation types | Identifies multiple types of disconfirming evidence |
| Search locations | Specifies realistic places to find counter-evidence |
| Publication bias awareness | Recognizes why disconfirming evidence is often missing |
| Steel man quality | Counter-argument is genuinely strong, not a straw man |
| Steel man evidence | Provides real evidence for the counter-argument |
| Honest engagement | Takes the counter-argument seriously |

### Common Mistakes

- Weak counter-arguments that are easy to dismiss
- Ignoring publication bias and file-drawer effects
- Looking for disconfirming evidence only where it won't be found
- Dismissing the steel man without engaging with its merit
- Treating adversarial thinking as merely procedural
